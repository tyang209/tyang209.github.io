What do I do?


Kaleidoscope

Kaleidoscope is a weekly improv show I host and book at the Creek in the Cave. Every week three improvisers and I create teams and improvise off stories told by a monologist. It's a show that's been passed down from host to host for the last SEVEN years!!!

High Noon

I write on the sketch team High Noon. We perform a monthly show at the Pit Loft as part of the Boogiemanja sketch collective.

Terms of Service
